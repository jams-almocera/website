Product Details

The Apple Watch Ultra 2 is tailored for endurance athletes, outdoor adventurers, and water sport enthusiasts, boasting rugged durability and specialized bands for various activities. With the powerful S9 SiP, it offers a superbright display and intuitive touchless interaction. Featuring up to 36 hours of battery life and 100m water resistance, it ensures reliability in challenging environments while remaining carbon neutral when paired with Alpine or Trail Loop bands, reflecting Apple's commitment to sustainability. This incredibly capable smartwatch showcases a 49mm titanium case and the brightest Always-On Retina display yet, providing ample room for customization and easy visibility even in sunlight. With built-in cellular connectivity, users enjoy freedom from their iPhones, whether calling, texting, or streaming music overseas. Tailored features for athletes include advanced workout metrics and dual-frequency GPS, while outdoor adventurers benefit from the Compass app's detailed views and backtrack capabilities, alongside rugged bands like the Alpine Loop for added security and durability.
