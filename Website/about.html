Who Are We?


At TechThrift, we're passionate about making technology accessible to everyone. As a small team
of two developers, both students pursuing a Bachelor of Science in Computer Science at Arellano
University, we're dedicated to offering a wide range of tech products, from vintage gadgets to
cutting-edge innovations. Our mission is to provide our customers with affordable options, akin to a
thrift shop experience, where they can find high-quality tech at lower prices. With our combined
expertise and enthusiasm for technology, we strive to create a platform that caters to the diverse
needs and interests of tech enthusiasts and bargain hunters alike. Join us at TechThrift and discover
a treasure trove of tech wonders waiting to be explored!
